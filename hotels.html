Hotels

These are most of the hotels in Grand Cayman that are worth visiting if your staying, or you are a tourist.

  1. First hotel we have in store is the Kimpton Seafire Resort + Spa. This is a great hotel because its rated.... 5 stars. Very interesting hotel.
  2. Secondly, we have the Ritz - Carlton. The Ritz - Carlton is a nice hotel with a tennis court, helipad, and so much more. But it is quite expensive. It is worth the money if your visiting.
  3. Then we have Palm Heights. 4.8 Star hotel with pools and every thing you would need for your stay. I have never been here so try it out for yourself!
  4. The Westin Is a great hotel as it has a gym, beach, pool, and a mini bar! What else could you ask for?
  5. The Morrits has a way to make visitors come back to their hotel for over 30 years. Located in the laid-back cultural district of East End, Morritt's is a unique property that is a relaxing retreat from the fast pace of life. Here you will find a peaceful haven with white sand beaches and spectacular ocean views, along with some of the world's best scuba diving and snorkelling. With on-site pools, restaurants, spa, and access to a variety of watersports activities
