Hotels

These are most of the hotels in Grand Cayman that are worth visiting if your staying, or you are a tourist.

  1. First hotel we have in store is the Kimpton Seafire Resort + Spa. This is a great hotel because it is rated.... 5 stars. Very interesting hotel.
  2. Secondly, we have the Ritz - Carlton. The name speaks for itself.A very high quality hotel with a tennis court, helipad, and so much more. But it is quite expensive. It is worth the money if your visiting.
  3. Then we have Palm Heights. 4.8 Star hotel with pools and every thing you would need for your stay. I have never been here so try it out for yourself!
  4. The Westin is a great hotel as it has a gym, beach, pool, and a mini bar! What else could you ask for?
  5. The Morrits has a way to make visitors come back to their hotel for over 30 years. the Morrits has the best scuba diving and snorkelling. With on-site pools, restaurants, spa, and access to a variety of watersports activities
  6. The cheapest hotel I could find was for 197 dollars (Sunshine Suites Resort). Yea, not the best. But there are more options.
