These are activities that can be done in Cayman. For tourists and Caymanians.

  • You can go to Pedro St. James, Price for Pedro St. James : $25.00 for a tour.
  • You can go visit Starfish point! : Free if you own a rental, car, or boat!
  • Go to Cayman Turtle Centre
  • Have fun at Seven Mile Beach
  • Go to the Cayman Crystal Caves and learn its history
  • See Queen Elizabeth II's Botanic Park. Price : For vistors its $20.00 / Kids are $10.00. For residents its $10.00 for adults and $5.00 for kids.
  • Go visit Rum Point! Price: Free if you own a boat, but there is a restaurant there.
  • Visit Caymana Bay and eat some ice cream or if you feel like you've got time, visit the movie theater. Price: Free (If your just wondering around.)
  • Explore the sunken Kittiwake Shipwreck if you feel brave... Price: Free if you own a boat but the price depends on your group size if you need a tour guide.
  • Go chill out at Governors beach and splash around! Price: Free because its a beach.
  • Go kiss a stingray if you feel lucky! Price: Free if you own a boat!
